    why use a broker

    Yacht Brokers work like real estate agents.




    They are agents with whom people consult to find and purchase a boat, and whom people hire to list, represent, and sell boats for them. Traditionally, the seller pays the commissions that a yacht broker earns not the buyer. Yet, brokers have a duty to both buyer and seller in every transaction.
